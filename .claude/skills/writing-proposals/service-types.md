# Energy Consulting Service Types & Scope Templates

Detailed scope definitions for energy consulting service offerings.

## 1. Benchmarking & Data Verification

### Description
Annual energy performance data submission to comply with building performance program requirements.

### Typical Clients
- Building owners required to benchmark
- Property managers managing portfolios
- First-time benchmarking submitters
- Buildings needing data verification assistance

### Scope of Work

**Task 1: Data Collection & Verification**
- Collect 12 months of utility data (electricity, gas, water)
- Verify billing periods align with calendar year
- Identify and resolve data gaps or inconsistencies
- Coordinate with utility companies if needed

**Task 2: Energy Star Portfolio Manager Setup/Update**
- Set up new property in Portfolio Manager (if needed)
- Update building characteristics (sq ft, occupancy, hours, etc.)
- Input monthly utility data
- Calculate weather-normalized EUI
- Generate Portfolio Manager score (if eligible)

**Task 3: Compliance Submission**
- Compile required data fields per program specifications
- Submit benchmarking report via required portal
- Submit data verification documents (utility bills, etc.)
- Retain confirmation of submission for client records

**Task 4: Reporting**
- Provide summary report to client showing:
  - Current EUI vs. performance targets (if applicable)
  - Portfolio Manager score and percentile ranking
  - Year-over-year energy use comparison
  - Recommendations for improving performance (optional)

### Deliverables
- Energy Star Portfolio Manager property setup/update
- Compliance submission confirmation
- Summary report with EUI and performance analysis
- Copy of all data verification documents

### Timeline
- Standard: 2-4 weeks
- Rush (for program deadlines): 1-2 weeks (if data readily available)

### Typical Pricing
- **Small buildings (25k-50k sf)**: $2,000-$3,500
- **Medium buildings (50k-100k sf)**: $3,500-$5,000
- **Large/complex buildings (100k+ sf)**: $5,000-$8,000
- **Portfolio (multiple buildings)**: Volume discount available

### Pricing Model
- **Fixed Fee**: Most common for straightforward buildings
- **Time & Materials with NTE**: For buildings with data quality issues

## 2. ASHRAE Level 1 Energy Audit (Walk-Through)

### Description
Preliminary assessment identifying low-cost/no-cost energy conservation measures and operational improvements.

### Typical Clients
- Buildings starting compliance pathway
- Owners seeking quick operational wins
- Properties with limited budget for analysis

### Scope of Work

**Task 1: Pre-Audit Data Collection**
- Review utility bills (24 months)
- Review benchmarking data and EUI trends
- Obtain building plans and equipment schedules (if available)
- Coordinate site visit with building engineer/manager

**Task 2: Walk-Through Assessment**
- Survey HVAC systems, controls, and operations
- Inspect lighting systems and controls
- Evaluate envelope conditions (windows, insulation, air sealing)
- Review domestic hot water systems
- Interview operations staff on current practices
- Identify operational issues and low-cost improvements

**Task 3: Energy Analysis**
- Calculate current energy use breakdown by end use (HVAC, lighting, plug loads)
- Identify low-cost and no-cost ECMs
- Estimate energy savings for each ECM (order of magnitude)
- Provide qualitative cost estimates (low/medium/high)

**Task 4: Report & Recommendations**
- ASHRAE Level 1 Audit Report including:
  - Building characteristics and current energy use
  - Identified ECMs with savings estimates
  - Prioritized recommendations
  - Potential path to meeting performance targets (if applicable)
  - Recommendations for Level 2 audit (if needed)

### Deliverables
- ASHRAE Level 1 Energy Audit Report
- ECM summary table with energy and cost savings
- Photos of existing conditions
- Recommendations for next steps

### Timeline
- Standard: 3-5 weeks from kickoff to final report

### Typical Pricing
- **Small buildings (25k-50k sf)**: $5,000-$8,000
- **Medium buildings (50k-100k sf)**: $8,000-$12,000
- **Large/complex buildings (100k+ sf)**: $12,000-$20,000

### Pricing Model
- **Fixed Fee**: Standard approach

## 3. ASHRAE Level 2 Energy Audit (Detailed Analysis)

### Description
Comprehensive engineering analysis with energy modeling, detailed ECM cost/benefit analysis, and implementation roadmap.

### Typical Clients
- Buildings developing compliance pathways for performance targets
- Owners planning capital improvements
- Properties applying for alternate compliance or incentive programs

### Scope of Work

**Task 1: Data Collection & Analysis**
- Detailed utility data analysis (3 years)
- Benchmarking and performance target review
- Obtain and review all building documentation (plans, specs, O&M manuals)
- Interview facility staff on operations and issues

**Task 2: Comprehensive Site Assessment**
- Multi-day site visit with engineering team
- Detailed survey of all major energy-using systems:
  - HVAC equipment (chillers, boilers, AHUs, terminal units)
  - Controls and building automation systems
  - Lighting and lighting controls
  - Envelope (windows, insulation, air leakage)
  - Plug loads and process equipment
  - Domestic hot water systems
- Spot measurements and trend data collection
- Equipment nameplate data collection

**Task 3: Energy Modeling**
- Develop calibrated baseline energy model (eQuest or EnergyPlus)
- Validate model against 12 months utility data
- Create ECM energy models for each measure
- Calculate energy savings for each ECM

**Task 4: Financial Analysis**
- Develop detailed cost estimates for each ECM (±30%)
- Calculate simple payback, NPV, IRR for each ECM
- Identify utility incentives and rebates
- Create ECM implementation packages (bundled measures)

**Task 5: Compliance Pathway (if applicable)**
- Verify current baseline EUI vs. performance targets
- Model compliance scenarios:
  - Operational improvements only
  - Capital improvements + operations
  - Renewable energy + improvements
- Recommend phased implementation strategy
- Evaluate alternate compliance pathways if needed

**Task 6: Report & Presentation**
- Comprehensive ASHRAE Level 2 Audit Report including:
  - Executive summary
  - Current building performance and compliance status
  - Detailed ECM descriptions with energy and cost analysis
  - Recommended compliance pathway with phasing (if applicable)
  - Implementation considerations
  - Energy model documentation (appendix)
- Presentation to ownership team

### Deliverables
- ASHRAE Level 2 Energy Audit Report (50-100 pages)
- ECM cost/benefit analysis summary
- Calibrated energy model files
- Compliance pathway roadmap (if applicable)
- Presentation slides and meeting

### Timeline
- Standard: 8-12 weeks from kickoff to final report
- Rush available: 6-8 weeks (premium pricing)

### Typical Pricing
- **Small buildings (25k-50k sf)**: $15,000-$25,000
- **Medium buildings (50k-100k sf)**: $25,000-$40,000
- **Large buildings (100k-200k sf)**: $40,000-$60,000
- **Very large/complex (200k+ sf)**: $60,000-$100,000+

**Pricing Factors**:
- Building size and complexity
- Number of major systems/equipment
- Energy modeling complexity
- Number of ECMs evaluated (typically 10-20)
- Compliance pathway complexity

### Pricing Model
- **Fixed Fee**: Most common
- **Tiered by Building Size**: Alternative approach

## 4. ASHRAE Level 3 Energy Audit (Investment Grade)

### Description
Detailed engineering analysis with guaranteed savings calculations, M&V protocols, and contractor-ready specifications.

### Typical Clients
- Buildings pursuing ESCO or performance contracting
- Owners requiring guaranteed savings for financing
- Projects requiring detailed design for implementation

### Scope of Work

Includes all Level 2 tasks PLUS:

**Task 1: Detailed Engineering Design**
- Develop conceptual designs for major ECMs
- Create detailed specifications (CSI format)
- Develop control sequences for HVAC improvements
- Create single-line diagrams for electrical upgrades

**Task 2: Enhanced Financial Analysis**
- Refine cost estimates to ±20% accuracy
- Develop guaranteed savings calculations
- Create measurement & verification (M&V) protocols per IPMVP
- Model cash flows for financing scenarios

**Task 3: Implementation Planning**
- Develop phased construction schedule
- Identify contractor qualifications
- Create procurement strategy
- Establish project risks and mitigation strategies

**Task 4: Comprehensive Documentation**
- Investment Grade Audit Report (100+ pages)
- Bid-ready specifications for each ECM
- M&V plan for savings verification
- Financial pro forma for board approval

### Deliverables
- ASHRAE Level 3 Investment Grade Audit Report
- ECM specifications and design documents
- Guaranteed savings calculations with M&V protocols
- Implementation and procurement plans
- Financial analysis and cash flow models

### Timeline
- Standard: 12-16 weeks from kickoff to final report

### Typical Pricing
- **Medium buildings (50k-100k sf)**: $40,000-$70,000
- **Large buildings (100k-200k sf)**: $70,000-$120,000
- **Very large/complex (200k+ sf)**: $120,000-$200,000+

### Pricing Model
- **Fixed Fee**: Standard
- **Percentage of Construction Cost**: Alternative (2-4% of estimated ECM costs)

## 5. Compliance Pathway Consulting

### Description
Strategic consulting to develop multi-year plan for meeting building performance program targets.

### Typical Clients
- Buildings behind on compliance or at risk of missing targets
- Owners seeking least-cost compliance strategy
- Buildings considering alternate compliance pathways

### Scope of Work

**Task 1: Compliance Status Assessment**
- Review benchmarking history and current EUI
- Verify baseline EUI and performance targets
- Analyze energy use trends
- Identify compliance gaps for interim and final targets

**Task 2: Pathway Analysis**
- Model compliance scenarios:
  - Operational improvements (no capital)
  - Low-cost capital improvements
  - Major retrofits
  - Renewable energy procurement
  - Combination strategies
- Evaluate alternate compliance pathways:
  - Timing adjustments
  - Target adjustments for building use
  - Prescriptive options

**Task 3: Cost-Benefit Analysis**
- Estimate costs for each pathway option
- Calculate avoided penalties for compliance
- Compare NPV of different strategies
- Identify utility incentives and rebates

**Task 4: Implementation Roadmap**
- Recommend optimal compliance strategy
- Develop phased action plan
- Identify key decision points and milestones
- Outline next steps and resource needs

**Task 5: Alternate Compliance Application (if applicable)**
- Prepare application for regulatory review
- Document justification for adjustment
- Coordinate submission and follow-up

### Deliverables
- Compliance Pathway Strategy Report
- Cost-benefit analysis for pathway options
- Multi-year implementation roadmap
- Alternate compliance application (if applicable)

### Timeline
- Standard: 6-8 weeks from kickoff to final report
- Alternate compliance application: Add 2-4 weeks for regulatory coordination

### Typical Pricing
- **Basic pathway (no alternate compliance)**: $10,000-$18,000
- **Complex pathway with multiple scenarios**: $18,000-$28,000
- **Alternate compliance application**: Add $5,000-$10,000

### Pricing Model
- **Fixed Fee**: Standard approach

## 6. Performance Target Analysis

### Description
Focused analysis to establish or verify baseline EUI and calculate performance targets for building performance program compliance.

### Typical Clients
- New covered buildings establishing first targets
- Buildings with missing or questionable baseline data
- Buildings changing use type requiring target recalculation

### Scope of Work

**Task 1: Baseline EUI Verification**
- Review historical benchmarking data
- Verify building type classification
- Identify data quality issues or anomalies
- Coordinate with regulatory agency if baseline dispute needed

**Task 2: Performance Target Calculation**
- Calculate or verify interim and final performance targets
- Model impact of renewable energy credits on EUI
- Compare targets to industry benchmarks
- Assess feasibility of meeting targets

**Task 3: Data Quality Improvement**
- Identify missing or incorrect data in Portfolio Manager
- Correct building characteristics for accurate benchmarking
- Ensure proper meter boundary accounting
- Re-run Portfolio Manager calculations

**Task 4: Reporting & Recommendations**
- Summary report with verified baseline and targets
- Data quality improvement recommendations
- Initial assessment of compliance difficulty
- Recommendations for next steps (audit, pathway analysis, etc.)

### Deliverables
- Baseline EUI and Performance Target Report
- Corrected Energy Star Portfolio Manager property
- Data quality assessment and recommendations

### Timeline
- Standard: 2-4 weeks from kickoff to final report

### Typical Pricing
- **Straightforward analysis**: $3,000-$5,000
- **Complex (multiple buildings or baseline dispute)**: $5,000-$8,000

### Pricing Model
- **Fixed Fee**: Standard approach

## Proposal Customization Notes

When generating proposals:

1. **Service Description**: Use templates above but customize for specific building situation
2. **Scope Tasks**: Include/exclude tasks based on client needs and building complexity
3. **Deliverables**: Adjust list to match scope
4. **Timeline**: Consider:
   - Building size and complexity
   - Data availability
   - Client responsiveness
   - Compliance deadlines (program-specific)
   - Internal workload and scheduling
5. **Pricing**: Use ranges above as starting point, adjust for:
   - Building complexity factors
   - Data quality and availability
   - Timeline urgency
   - Portfolio/multi-building discounts
   - Competitive landscape
